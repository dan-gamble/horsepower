Nearly everything here was shot on a 2005 Nikon D2Hs. A few photos were taken with a 2006 Nikon D200, and a few were taken with a 2004 Canon EOS 1D Mark II.

I used an 18-70mm DX lens on the Nikon cameras for most photographs. Low-light photos usually use the Nikon 50mm f/1.8 D. All photographs from the EOS 1D were with Canon's cheapest 50mm f/1.8 EF lens.
